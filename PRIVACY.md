# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 30, 2026

## Overview
Snake (Aviator) is a mobile and desktop game that respects your privacy. We collect minimal data and do not share your information with third parties.

## Data Collection
- **High Scores**: Stored locally on your device (localStorage/app storage) — NOT sent to servers
- **No Analytics**: We do not track gameplay or user behavior
- **No Ads**: No third-party ad networks or trackers
- **No Login**: No account creation or authentication required

## Device Permissions
- **Audio**: Used only for in-game sound effects and music (user-controlled toggle)
- **Storage**: Local saves only (high scores, game state)
- **Camera/Location**: Never accessed

## Third-Party Services
- **CDN/Hosting**: If hosted online, uses standard web server logs (no tracking pixels)
- **No Cookies**: No cookies or tracking technologies
